Embedded Payroll

Adding embedded payroll to your platform is a technology integration project — and the Canadian government actively funds exactly this kind of work. Your development costs, compliance engineering, and API integration work may qualify for non-repayable grants, tax credits, and interest-free financing that can offset 40–75% of total project costs.

Programs That Fund Payroll Integration

SR&ED Tax Credit

Tax Credit
Up to $1.4M/year in credits
Canada's largest R&D incentive. The enhanced rate gives CCPCs a 35% refundable credit on the first $4M of eligible R&D expenditure (Budget 2025 increase). Building payroll compliance logic, provincial tax engines, and API integrations involves genuine technical uncertainty — the kind SR&ED was designed for.
Why Payroll Integration Qualifies

Developing embedded payroll requires solving technical challenges: multi-provincial tax calculation engines, regulatory compliance automation (ROEs, T4s, stat holidays), real-time CRA reporting, and money movement integrations. This is technology development, not routine software configuration.

Status: Open year-round
Eligibility: Canadian-controlled private corporations
Claim: Retroactive (file with tax return)

NRC IRAP

Non-Repayable
Avg. $500K per project
Canada's flagship innovation program funds up to 80% of R&D labour costs for SMEs building new technology. Covers proof-of-concept through working prototype (TRL 3–7). Over 3,100 companies funded annually from a $437M budget. You also get a dedicated Industrial Technology Advisor — free.
Why Payroll Integration Qualifies

Integrating payroll into a vertical SaaS platform requires novel architecture — embedding compliance engines, building multi-tenant payroll data models, and creating seamless UX for an entirely new product vertical. This is exactly the technology innovation IRAP funds.

Status: Open continuously
Eligibility: Incorporated, <500 employees
Timeline: 3–5 months to approval

ACOA — Regional Economic Growth (REGI)

Grant / Loan
Project-based funding
For Atlantic Canada businesses adopting innovative technologies and scaling operations. Specifically funds technology integration, new product development, and market expansion. ACOA just announced $8.5M in new AI/tech adoption funding (March 2026).
Why Payroll Integration Qualifies

Adopting embedded payroll is a technology integration project that expands your product offering and opens new markets. If you're based in NS, NB, PEI, or NL — this is designed for you.

Status: Open continuously
Eligibility: Atlantic Canada businesses, all sizes
Provinces: NS, NB, PEI, NL

How Programs Stack Together

Combined Coverage Up to 75% of total project costs (government stacking cap)

What Integration Work Qualifies

Typical eligible activities when adding embedded payroll:
✅ API integration development (connecting your platform to payroll engines)
✅ Multi-provincial compliance logic (tax rules, stat holidays, ROEs)
✅ Payroll UI/UX design and development within your platform
✅ Data architecture for multi-tenant payroll (security, isolation, performance)
✅ Automated CRA reporting and year-end processing (T4s, RL-1s)
✅ Money movement and banking integration development
✅ QA and compliance testing across provinces
✅ Prototype and proof-of-concept development
